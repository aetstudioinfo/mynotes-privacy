GDPR — European Union Users

🇪🇺 GDPR · EU / EEA / UK
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies to our processing of your personal data.

Legal bases for processing:

  • Consent — Cloud backup and personalised advertising. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Contract performance — Core note-taking functionality and in-app purchases.
  • Legitimate interest — App preferences and security (PIN protection).

Your GDPR rights:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to rectification — Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — Request that we limit processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at aetstudioinfo@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

CCPA — California Residents

🇺🇸 CCPA · California
If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration.

Your CCPA rights:

  • Right to know — Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to delete — Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to opt-out — As stated above, we do not sell personal information. No opt-out action is needed.
  • Right to non-discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To submit a CCPA request, email aetstudioinfo@gmail.com with the subject line "CCPA Request". We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

Advertising & Consent (AdMob / UMP)

📢 AdMob · UMP · GDPR Compliant
The free version of MyNotes displays banner ads via Google AdMob. If you are in the EEA or UK, a consent dialog (Google's User Messaging Platform — UMP) is shown before any personalised advertising data is collected.

How it works:

  • On first launch in EEA/UK regions, a consent screen asks whether you agree to personalised ads.
  • If you consent, AdMob may use your Advertising ID (GAID) to show personalised ads.
  • If you decline, only non-personalised ads are shown and your GAID is not used for ad targeting.
  • You can update your ad preferences at any time via Device Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Purchase "Remove Ads" to permanently disable all advertisements and ad tracking.

Google AdMob privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Firebase & Cloud Backup

When you enable cloud backup:

  • You authenticate with your Google account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Your notes are uploaded to Firebase Firestore in JSON format.
  • This data belongs solely to you; no other users can access it.
  • You can delete this data at any time by signing out or contacting us.

Firebase data processing policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

🔔

Notifications & Permissions

  • Notification permission: Required for reminders and pinned notes.
  • Exact alarm permission (Android 12+): Required for timed reminders.
  • Internet access: Required for ad loading and cloud backup.

These permissions are optional. Denying a permission only disables the related feature; all other functions remain unaffected.

Data Security

  • Notes are stored locally using Android DataStore.
  • Individual notes can be locked with a PIN protected by SHA-256 hashing.
  • Cloud data is secured within Firebase's infrastructure.
  • We do not sell or rent your data to any third parties.

Reklamlar & Rıza (AdMob / UMP)

📢 AdMob · UMP · GDPR Uyumlu
MyNotes'un ücretsiz sürümü Google AdMob aracılığıyla banner reklamlar gösterir. AB/AEA veya Birleşik Krallık'ta bulunuyorsanız, kişiselleştirilmiş reklam verisi toplanmadan önce Google'ın Kullanıcı Mesajlaşma Platformu (UMP) aracılığıyla bir rıza ekranı gösterilir.

Nasıl çalışır:

  • AB/AEA/UK bölgelerinde ilk açılışta kişiselleştirilmiş reklam için rıza ekranı gösterilir.
  • Rıza verirseniz AdMob, reklam tanımlayıcınızı (GAID) kişiselleştirilmiş reklam için kullanabilir.
  • Rıza vermezseniz yalnızca kişiselleştirilmemiş reklamlar gösterilir; GAID hedefleme için kullanılmaz.
  • Reklam tercihlerinizi Cihaz Ayarları → Google → Reklamlar üzerinden istediğiniz zaman güncelleyebilirsiniz.
  • "Reklamları Kaldır" satın alımıyla tüm reklamları ve reklam takibini kalıcı olarak devre dışı bırakabilirsiniz.

Google AdMob gizlilik politikası: policies.google.com/privacy
